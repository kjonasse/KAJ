Who Am I and What Can I Do For You

My name is Kimberly Jonasse and I am a freelance graphic designer / web developer based in Harrisburg, NC - right outside of Charlotte. Starting off as a graphic designer in 1999, I have recently jumped into the world of digital design by completing the Full Stack Web Development Program at UNCC. I have since been creating user-friendly digital designs ranging from web to mobile. I am always trying to stay up to date on new digital technologies and latest design practices. I am currently taking on new projects - please contact me to get started!
